Harry Styles announced a seven-city 2026 residency called Together, Together that comprises 50 shows across seven cities. It is his first tour in three years. He released a new single, Aperture, on January 22, 2026. His fourth album, KISS ALL THE TIME. DISCO, OCCASIONALLY, is set for release on March 6, 2026.

Tour schedule and venues

50
Total shows
Seven-city residency in 2026

New York's run will include 30 shows at Madison Square Garden. Forbes and the Lancashire Post, citing an official statement from Styles, reported a six-night stint at Wembley Stadium beginning on June 12. By contrast, the Daily Mail reported the London shows as a six-night run at Wembley Arena.

  • Amsterdam
  • London
  • São Paulo
  • Mexico City
  • New York
  • Melbourne
  • Sydney

The itinerary spans Europe, the Americas and Australia.

Charity, civic and sustainability commitments

Styles said he would donate £1 from every ticket sold on the London dates to LIVE Trust. Styles said the New York dates will partner with HeadCount to offer on-site voter registration for eligible fans. Tour organizers said the global trek will support Choose Love, a humanitarian group that provides aid to refugees and displaced people. Live Nation said its Green Nation initiative will work to lessen the tour's environmental impact across the dates.

Reactions and speculation

Fans flooded X with ecstatic responses, describing the announcement as a long-awaited return and posting celebratory messages. Some fans also commented that Styles's accent sounded more American than before.

Styles said he needed to fall in love with music all over again after his break. He described the shows as intended to focus on shared fun and being together rather than a one-way stage dynamic. The Daily Mail speculated that the release timing could produce a chart confrontation with One Direction bandmate Louis Tomlinson.

It happened, I was getting a haircut in Rome
— Harry Styles

He recalled crowds shouting Habemus Papam in Rome, saying people started running down the street and the barber stopped cutting his hair.

State of play

Across reporting, the confirmed elements are that Together, Together is a seven-city, 50-show residency. The project is tied to Styles's new single, Aperture, released on January 22, 2026, and to his fourth album, KISS ALL THE TIME. DISCO, OCCASIONALLY, due on March 6, 2026. Styles and organizers have announced charitable and sustainability partners, including a pledged £1 from London ticket sales and support for Choose Love and Live Nation's Green Nation initiative, but outlets disagree on whether the London dates will be at Wembley Stadium or at Wembley Arena, and it remains speculative whether the timing will provoke a chart confrontation, as the Daily Mail suggested.
