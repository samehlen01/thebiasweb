Adrian Gonzales Acquitted in Uvalde Case

A jury in Corpus Christi acquitted former Uvalde school officer Adrian Gonzales on all 29 counts of child abandonment or endangerment after deliberating for about seven hours. The trial centred on competing accounts — prosecutors said officers abandoned training and failed to confront the shooter, while Gonzales's defense pointed to body-camera footage that it said shows him trying to reach the attacker — and reporting also diverged on how many officers responded to Robb Elementary.
